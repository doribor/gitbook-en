# Plans

Wfolio offers two options: **Site + Drive** or **Drive Only**.

With **Site + Drive**, you get a **fully functional website** where you can showcase your **portfolio, client reviews, blog, pricing details, contact information,** and more.

Or use **Drive Only**—a powerful tool for creating **client galleries**, displaying files in a personalized and visually appealing format. Wfolio galleries are designed to be both **elegant and user-friendly**.

## Plan options

### Site + Drive plan

* **Standard:** Site + Cloud drive 120 GB — $4.99/month or $49.99/year.
* **Unlim:** Site + Unlimited cloud drive for JPEG (other files are limited to 120 GB) — $9.49/month or $94.99/year.

### Drive Only plan

* [**Free 120 GB plan**](free-plan.md) for a post on social media.
* **240 GB** (non JPEG files are limited to 120 GB) — $2,90/month or $29,90/year.
* **1000 GB** cloud drive for JPEG (other files are limited to 120 GB) — $5,90/month or $59,90/year.
* **Unlim** for JPEG (other files are limited to 120 GB) — $9,90/month or $99,90/year.

#### Site + Drive with the White Label option

Available after a plan without options is paid.

* **Standard + White Label:** Site without a label `site by wfolio` at the page footer + Cloud drive 120 GB — $5.99/month or $59.99/year.
* **Unlim + White Label :** Site without a label `site by wfolio` at the page footer + Unlimited cloud drive for JPEG (other files are limited to 120 GB) — $10.49/month or $109.49/year.

## Promo code discount

When you sign up, a **promo code for a discount** is automatically applied. If you have a separate **referral promo code**, enter it during registration.&#x20;

You can find details about your discount in the **Plans & billing** section of the settings and in the **payment window** when selecting a plan.

The promo code applies **only to the first annual payment** and can be used **once**. If you're paying **monthly**, the promo code remains active **until your first annual payment**.

For free plan extensions, use our [referral program](invite-friend.md).
