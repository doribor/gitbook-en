# Referral program

After signing up, you get access to the referral program. You receive a unique referral link—send it to a friend or share it on social media.

If a friend signs up using your link, they’ll receive **35% off their first annual payment**. And you’ll get **3 free bonus months** added to your plan for each friend who purchases any Wfolio annual plan.

You can share or change your referral link and view registration stats in the **Referral program** section of your settings.

### How to share your referral link in Stories

* Choose a ready-made template from Wfolio’s designers and download it using the provided link.
* Open your preferred social network and create a new Story.
* Add the downloaded template and insert your referral link.
* Publish your Story and start inviting friends!
