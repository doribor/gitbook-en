# Copying list in LR and C1

### Copy the list to Lightroom <a href="#lightroom" id="lightroom"></a>

You can copy the file list and paste it into Lightroom to edit your favorite photos.

How to copy a list to Lightroom:

* Go to the **Favorites** section in your project on **the drive**. Next to the list that you need, click on **the ellipsis** and select **Lightroom & Capture One**.
* Click on the **Copy** button in the opened window.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/capture one selection en.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Or copy the file lists from all selection.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/capture all selections en.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Open Adobe Lightroom Classic, import all the photos from the series. Click on the **Text** button, so the text input field will be opened on the right.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (342).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

In the search settings, select **File name** in the first drop-down list, and **Contains** in the second one. Then paste the copied list of files into **the search bar on the right**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (251).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Copy the list to Capture One <a href="#capture" id="capture"></a>

You can copy the file list and paste it into Capture One to edit your favorite photos.

How to copy a list to Capture One:

* Go to the **Favorites** section in your project on **the drive**. Next to the list that you need, click on **the ellipsis** and select **Lightroom & Capture One.**
* Click on the **Copy** button in the opened window.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/capture one selection en.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Or copy the file lists from all selection.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/capture all selections en.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Open Capture One, import all the photos from the series. Click on the **search icon** and paste the copied favorites list into **the search bar**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (423).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
