# QR code for galleries

Wfolio allows you to generate a QR code for your gallery and save it to your device for easy sharing.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/gallery qr en.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Get the QR code from the project list

In the project list on your drive, click the arrow next to the desired project and select **Download QR code**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/gallery get qr 1 en.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Get the QR code from inside the project

On the project page, go to the **Share project** option and select **Download QR code**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/gallery get qr 2 en.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
