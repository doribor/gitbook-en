# Gallery design

You can customize the appearance of your gallery to better match the style of the shoot or your client’s preferences. To do this, open the project and go to the **Design and Cover** tab.

To set up your cover photo, see [cover.md](cover.md "mention").

### Color scheme

You can choose between a **light** or **dark** color scheme. The background under the photos, text color, and interface elements will adapt to the selected theme.

### Fonts

There are 6 font style presets available. Each preset is a combination of fonts for headings and body text.

* **Elegante**, **Universal**, and **Classic** preserve case formatting. If you name a folder in lowercase, it will be displayed as is.
* **Modern**, **Decorate**, and **Light** do not preserve case. All names will appear in uppercase.

### Spacing between photos

Spacing is adjustable for the **desktop version** only and can be set to **small**, **medium**, or **large**.

{% hint style="info" %}
On the phone, the spaces between photos are small and cannot be adjusted.
{% endhint %}

### Icons under the cover

You can customize how icons appear beneath the gallery cover:

* **Icons with labels.** Favorites, Review, Services.
* **Icons only.** Minimal layout without labels.
