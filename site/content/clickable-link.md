### Button link

On a [custom page](../edit-page/add-custom-page.md), click the <mark style="background-color:blue;">**+**</mark> button to add a new block. In the window that appears, open the **Buttons** tab and select the block option with a button that suits you.

#### Site page link

In the button settings, select **Open website page** and specify the page to which the transition will be made.

#### Redirect to an external address

Using the button, you can set up a redirect to an external address. For example, you can [add a download link](add-file.md) or a link to your blog or magazine publications.

In the button settings, select **Go to external address** and insert the link address.

#### Buttons for social networks and messengers

You can add stylish buttons with icons of social networks and messengers to your website pages, allowing visitors to go to your social media profiles or contact you.

In the button settings, select the action **Go to social network** (to add Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, Vimeo) or **Go to messenger** (to add WhatsApp, Telegram). From the dropdown list, choose a social network or messenger and enter the URL of your profile or your phone number.

{% hint style="info" %}
For WhatsApp, you can additionally set up an automatic message.
{% endhint %}

#### Phone number

In the button settings, select **Make a phone call** and enter the phone number.

#### Email

In the button settings, select **Go to external address** and insert the `mailto:youremail`, where youremail is your email address.
