# Slider block

Use the **Slider block** or the **Strip slider** block. Blocks are added to a [custom page](../edit-page/add-custom-page.md).

To add a slider:

* Open a custom page and press the <mark style="background-color:blue;">**+**</mark> button to add a neww block. Go to the **Image sliders** tab and select a block.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/add slider en.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Click on the **Upload** button to add photos to the slider.
* After loading, customize the view of the slider and arrange the photos in the order you need by dragging them with the mouse.
