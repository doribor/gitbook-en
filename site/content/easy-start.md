# How to build a website

Creating your website from scratch is easy, even for a beginner. This is a great way to showcase your work and grow your audience.&#x20;

Website-builder allows you to:

{% columns %}
{% column %}
* Display your portfolio.
* Use ready-to-go templates.
* Publish feedback from your clients.
* Accept online payment.
* Offer services, photobooks, courses, presets, certificates and more.
* Adapt your website for viewing on PCs and mobile devices.
* Share blog posts.
{% endcolumn %}

{% column %}
* Protect photos and videos from downloading.
* Set up password protection for your website's pages.
* Create versions for different languages.
* Use a personal domain.
* Collect data about traffic to your website.
* Add widgets.
{% endcolumn %}
{% endcolumns %}

With our guide you can quickly launch your website, customize it and promote it on social engines.

{% embed url="https://vimeo.com/1129807863" fullWidth="false" %}

**Your website is ready!**

Now choose a unique and eye catching [personal domain](../../domain/new.md#name) and share the link to your website on social media. You can also share photos with your clients via [**cloud galleries**](../../gallery/projects/easy-start.md) and promote your work through word of mouth.

{% hint style="success" %}
If you have any questions, contact our support team on the website. We will be happy to help.
{% endhint %}
