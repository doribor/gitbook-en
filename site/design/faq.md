# FAQ. Design

### I'm not a photographer or a videographer. Will your platform suit me?

Sure! Theme designs and page templates are universal. Just select the template option that suits you and insert your text and images.



### Can I change the theme of the website?

Yes, all the information on the website will be saved at the same time. You will only need to re-configure the colors and fonts on the wevsite.
