# How to delete a logotype

To remove the logo, go to the <img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (259).png" alt="" data-size="line"> [**Design**](https://wfolio.com/my/design) page in the **Logo** section and turn off the **Display Logo** switch.

A beautiful logo is an important element of the website and we strongly recommend that you do not refuse to use it.
