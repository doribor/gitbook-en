# How to highlight a menu item

If you need to highlight one or more menu items, then you can do it using special characters or emojis.

Add a page. Put one or more symbols or emojis in the name.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Frame 746.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

As a result, you have a highlighted item in the menu that attracts attention:

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Frame 747.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

You can add emojis and special characters to the text on the page:

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Frame 748.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

{% hint style="info" %}
Do not use special characters and emojis too often, they can worsen the perception of the website and make it more difficult.
{% endhint %}



### Special characters and emojis in the text and titles

You can add special symbols or emojis to titles and texts on your website. In order to do this, when editing text, press **Ctrl + Cmd** **+ Space (on Mac)** or **Win + «Period» (on Windows 10)** and in the opened window, select the symbol or emoji that you want.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (296).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>
