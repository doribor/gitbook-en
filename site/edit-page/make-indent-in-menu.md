# How to make an indent in the menu

If you need to separate menu items, then you can use the drop-down menu as a separator.

How to do that step-by-step:

* Click on the **"Add to the Site +"** button and select the **"Drop-down menu"** item In the <img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (292).png" alt="" data-size="line"> [**wfolio**](https://wfolio.com/my/site) control panel.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Frame 742.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Enter the symbol that will serve as a separator in the **"Title"** field. For example, **"â€”"**. Make sure that the **"Display in the site menu"** switch is enabled. Then click on the **"Add"** button.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Frame 743.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Move the drop-down menu to the place where you want to see the separator. Drag the menu item down or up.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Frame 744.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

As a result, you get a non-clickable indent in the menu that separates the menu items.
