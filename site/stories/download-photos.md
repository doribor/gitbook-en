### Where downloads can be enabled

Photo downloads can be allowed in **blog posts** and **announcement posts**.

### How to enable photo downloads

* When creating or editing a post, open the **page settings**.
* Enable the toggle **Allow photos to be downloaded** and save your changes.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/allow donsloads in blog en.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Once enabled, a **Download** button will appear on your photos:

* **On desktop** the button appears when you hover over a photo.
* **On mobile** the button appears either under the image (in the **Images** block) or in fullscreen mode (in the **Gallery** block).
