# Categories in reviews and posts

Categories allow you to combine reviews (or posts) into separate thematic groups within one page. Use categories to make it easier to find reviews (or posts) on your site. The same review (or post) can belong to several categories at the same time (but no more than 3 categories).

You can use categories for reviews (or posts) classification based on different criteria:

* By languages.
* By photo shooting locations.
* By occupations.
* By any other classification based on your needs.

### **Adding categories in reviews**

You can add a new category when creating or editing a review. To do this, in the editing window, go to the **Categories** tab and enter the name of the new category, after completing the entry put a **comma** or press **Enter**.

### **Adding categories in posts**

You can add a new category when creating or editing a post (story). To do this, in the editing window, go to the **Categories** tab and enter the name of the new category, after completing the entry, put a **comma** or press **Enter**.
