# How to connect Google Search Console

To connect the [**Google Search Console**](https://search.google.com/search-console/) you need:

* Go to [**Google Search Console**](https://search.google.com/search-console/) and sign in. On the page that opens, select **URL prefix** and enter the full address of your site, starting with `https://`.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/gsc 1.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Select the **HTML tag** item in the **Other confirmation methods** block, and copy the HTML tag (meta tag).

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (420).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* On Wfolio, go to the **Settings â†’ Webmaster Tools** page. **Paste the copied meta tag** into the **Google Search Console** field and **save the changes**.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/gsc connect en.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Return to the Google Search Console and click on the **Submit** button at the bottom of the block. Google will check for a meta tag on your site and verify your rights.
