# How to change the cover for social networks on a separate page

The cover for social networks is displayed when the link is transmitted via social networks or messengers. You can change it for a separate page like this:

Open the page for editing, click on **"Settings"** and go to the section **"SEO and social networks"**. Replace the image in the **"Page cover for social networks"** item.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Frame 840.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Frame 841.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

If you need the one image for all pages of the website, then in the <img src="../../.gitbook/assets/image (429).png" alt="" data-size="line"> [**Main Settings**](https://wfolio.com/my/settings/main) section, replace the image in the **"Cover for social networks"** item.
