# Q\&A (FAQ) widget

Use the Q\&A (FAQ) widget to create a list of common customer questions and answers.

* Click on the <mark style="background-color:blue;">**+**</mark> button to add a new block.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Frame 789.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Go to the **Widgets** tab and select any Q\&A block.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Frame 790 (1).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Fill out the list of questions and their corresponding answers.

This will help you display a well-organized and visually appealing FAQ section on your website.
