# Page cover

You can add a photo or video cover to any page of your website. It can be a single image, a single video, or a slider with multiple images. You can overlay text, a button, and navigation arrows to switch between images.

These covers can be added in any [website theme](../design/change-theme.md) except for the **Plain** theme.

{% hint style="info" %}
#### Recommended cover format

Most monitors have an aspect ratio close to **16:9**, while mobile phones are closer to **9:21**.\
Choose images with a similar aspect ratio, at least **2560px wide** and **1920px high**.\
