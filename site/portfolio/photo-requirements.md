# Photo requirements

Upload photos in large size, the site will automatically reduce them for different devices without quality loss.

We recommend uploading photos in **JPG** format with a size of **at least 2560px** on the long side. You can also upload **PNG** images with a size of **no more than 10 MB**.

To compress an image of an image in PNG format, you can use the website [https://tinypng.com](https://tinypng.com/).
