# Public Offer

Public Offer is a document helping to establish legal and trustworthy relationships with your clients.

In the document, outline its nature, product descriptions and pricing, payment and delivery methods, company info and contact details, terms of acceptance and cancellation policies, restrictions, and other important conditions.

{% hint style="warning" %}
**This article is not legal advice.** Consult a lawyer before publishing the agreement.
{% endhint %}



### How to publish a Public Offer

* Go to the **Settings → Legal settings**.
* Сlick on the **Add** button next to the **Public Offer** document.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/legal user agreement en.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Paste the text of the offer and save the changes.

<figure><img src="../.gitbook/assets/legal user agreement modal window example en.png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>



### Where will the links to the Public Offer be placed?

The offer will be published:

* **On the website** and **in client galleries** in the foote&#x72;_._
* **In online payment forms**—before submitting the form, visitors and clients will be able to read the offer and check a box to confirm they accept its terms.
