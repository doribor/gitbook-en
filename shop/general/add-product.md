# Add product card

You can sell products and services directly on your website by setting up payment options and an order form. It can be a physical item, a digital product, a consultation, a photoshoot request, etc.

### How to create a product card <a href="#add" id="add"></a>

* Go to the **Shop** and click on the **Add product**.
* Enter the product or service name and description, and upload images.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/add product en.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Select **payment method**:

1. **Online payment.** If you want to accept payments directly on the website. Donâ€™t forget to connect a [payment system](../../payments/systems.md).
2. **Cash payment.** A client will place an order, and you will contact them directly.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/select payment option en.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* Set the price for the product or service. You can also add multiple pricing options: see [#variations](add-product.md#variations "mention") below.
* Enable the **Allow quantity selection** toggle if you want customers to order more than one item at a time.
* Click **Add** to save the product.
* If needed, configure contact collection or post-order actions as explained below.

### Price variations for a product <a href="#variations" id="variations"></a>

Go to the **Price per variations** to set different prices depending on the version of the product or service.\
This could include different print sizes, service packages, etc.

You can add up to 7 variants.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/add product variations en.png" alt="" width="563"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

#### Redirect to a page

Enable the **Go to page** toggle and set up one of the options:

* **Go to an external address.** Paste any link (e.g. to a file, private Telegram chat, another website).
* **Go to page.** Choose one of your existing pages (available on plans with the website builder).
