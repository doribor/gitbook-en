## How to add a product to a gallery

* [Create a product card in the **Shop**](../general/add-product.md).
* Go to the project settings and in the **Products** tab enable the **Show products in the project** option.
* Click on the **Add product** button and select the products that will be displayed in the gallery.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Group 1 (9).png" alt="" width="493"><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

* **Save your changes.** Click on the **View on site** button to see how the products appear in the gallery.

Products will be displayed in each folder under all photos.

### How to share a link to a product

In the **Products** section of the store click on the arrow next to the product or service card. Copy the link or share it directly in the messenger.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/Group 1 (11).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

By clicking on the link you will be able to place an order without going to the gallery.
