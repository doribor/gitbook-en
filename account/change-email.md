# How to change email for notifications

Notifications from feedback forms are sent to the email address specified during registration by default.

To change the email for notifications without changing the main email, go to the <img src="../.gitbook/assets/image (403).png" alt="" data-size="line"> [Notifications](https://wfolio.com/my/settings/informers) section.

Specify a new email address for notifications and enable notifications via Telegram.
