# PayPal connection

[**PayPal**](https://paypal.com/) is a comprehensive solution for accepting payments on your website. Simply create a business account to start accepting payments.

{% hint style="info" %}
Currently, you can accept payments only through the PayPal wallet.
{% endhint %}



### Supported currencies <a href="#currencies" id="currencies"></a>

Supported currencies include EUR, USD, GBP, CHF, DKK, SEK, AUD, ILS, PLN, THB, CZK, CAD. Total currencies available—24.

<a href="https://developer.paypal.com/docs/reports/reference/paypal-supported-currencies/" class="button secondary">All currencies</a>

### How to connect PayPal

* [Sign up](https://www.paypal.com/us/webapps/mpp/account-selection) for PayPal with a **business** account.
* After an account registration, go to Wfolio control panel and in **Shop → Store settings** section select **PayPal**.
* Enter the email address to which you registered your business account. Click the **Add** button.
